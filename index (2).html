Key Insights & Recommendations

Data-driven career guidance and success factors analysis

Key Research Findings

  • Students with CGPA > 7.5 show 65% higher placement rates
  • 6+ technical skills provide 2.5x better placement chances
  • Internships increase placement probability by 70%
  • Backlogs have the strongest negative correlation with placement
  • Workshops and certifications correlate with 45% improvement

For Students

  • Maintain CGPA above 7.5 for optimal placement chances
  • Develop 6+ technical skills through courses and practice
  • Complete at least one internship before final year
  • Minimize backlogs and clear them as soon as possible
  • Participate in workshops and obtain relevant certifications
  • Improve communication skills through practice and training

For Institutions

  • Identify at-risk students early using predictive models
  • Provide targeted skill development programs
  • Facilitate internship opportunities and industry connections
  • Implement mandatory communication skills training
  • Create support systems for students with academic difficulties
  • Use data-driven approaches for placement optimization
